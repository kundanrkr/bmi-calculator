BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a numerical value derived from a person's weight and height. The score determines if a person is at his/her healthy weight or needs to shed or gain more kilos. A BMI calculator is widely used as a general tool to assess if a person has a healthy body weight proportional to their height.

BMI Categories

Obese - 30.0 and above
